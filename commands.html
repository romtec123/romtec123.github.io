2B2BOT Commands - Discord [PREFIX: >]


>help

The bot will DM you an embed with all of the commands

>setup

The bot will send instructions on how to set it up

>q/que/queue/prio/prioq

Recieve Information on 2b2t's queue

>status/uptime/ping

Recieve some of 2b2t's stats

>invite

The bot will DM you an invite to the 2B2BOT discord & an 0Auth2 link

>seen "username"

Recieve when a user was last seen online

>stats "username"

Recieve a players 2b2t stats

>coords "server prefix"

Get the coords of the bot (Disabled on 2b2t)

>lookup "username"

Recieve all of the information 2B2BOT has on a player

>rps "hand"

Play rock paper scissors with the bot.

>8ball "question"

Let the bot answer your question.

>coinflip "OPTIONAL side"

Make the bot flip a coin

>roll "OPTIONAL side"

Make the roll a die

>stalker "add/remove/test"

Recive a message when a player joins 2B2T


2B2BOT Commands - Minecraft [PREFIX: !]


!help

Recieve a link to this page and the discord.

!ping "username"

Recieve your or the specified players ping.

!bestping/worstping

Recieve who has the best or worst ping on the server

!seen "username"

Recieve when a user was last seen online

!q/que/queue/prio/prioq/priority

Recieve 2b2t's queue information

!tps

Recieve the server's average and calulated TPS

!coords

Get the bot's coords (Disabled on 2B2T)

!follow

If the bot can see you, it will start to follow you

!stopfollow
